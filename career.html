Career

When I grow up, I want to be an endocrinologist! Endocrinology is a medical profession surrounding the endocrine system and its hormones. Imbalances in hormone levels results in diseases only curable by an endocrinologist. That's where I come in! I'll be able to diagnosis and prescribe medicine to treat the disease, which are impossible to cure without correcting the hormone imbalance. Some examples of these diseases are diabetes (insulin) and growth hormone deficiency.
